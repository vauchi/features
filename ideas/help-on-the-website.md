# Website Explainer Video

## Goal
Short animated video explaining vauchi's core concept for the landing page.

## Animaker Prompt (Draft)

> Create a 60-second explainer video in a friendly, minimalist cartoon style.
>
> **Scene 1 (Problem - 15s):**
> Show a person changing jobs. They update their email on their phone. Cut to frustrated friends trying to reach them at the old email, messages bouncing. Show the awkward "hey, here's my new info" text chain. Convey: keeping contacts updated is broken.
>
> **Scene 2 (Solution - 20s):**
> Two people meet at a coffee shop. They each show a QR code on their phones, scan each other. A "card" animates flying between phones. Later, one person updates their phone number - the change ripples out to everyone who has their card automatically. No action needed. Magic.
>
> **Scene 3 (Trust - 15s):**
> Show the two phones with a lock icon between them. Text: "End-to-end encrypted. Only you and your contacts." Show no server/cloud in the middle - emphasize peer-to-peer nature. Your data stays yours.
>
> **Scene 4 (CTA - 10s):**
> App icon, "Vauchi - Contact cards that stay current." Download buttons for iOS/Android.
>
> Style: Clean, modern, slightly playful. Muted colors with one accent color (the vauchi brand color). No voiceover, just music and text overlays.

## Key Messages to Convey
1. Contact info goes stale → vauchi fixes that
2. Exchange happens in-person (QR) → establishes trust
3. Updates propagate automatically → zero friction
4. Privacy-first, encrypted → you control your data
